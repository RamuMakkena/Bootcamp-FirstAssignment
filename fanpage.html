The course which made me to rethink

Hello All! Ramu Makkena here. Completed masters in computer applications ( ? ) more than a decade ago. Having 10+ years of experience in software industry. Currently holding a job title "Software Development Engineer" in Amazon. That ignites the question "Why coding bootcamp for you?", yes that is the question I am answering myself as

After completing masters degree, I have not pursued the path of developing softwares. Instead choose a job in software company. Later realized that company is a service based company where we have no choice of choosing a path to move forward than following the path laid by company requirements. That path kept me away from basic coding for close to a decade.

Then in Amazon, I found the path to code again. All good right? No, I am just coding to fix the current software, taking more than usual time to build something. My approach and designs are decaode old (obviously :) ) Now I realized this might put me in a position, where I will be in a position where I can hate myself to be in a job again where there is no coding involved. Finally took this step to go back to basics and be a student again to learn and develop software applications.

Agenda

I hope I will re-learn the below things to keep me active not become lazy

  • Back to basics
  • Reignite Fire to Code
  • Be a better coder
  • Do my job
